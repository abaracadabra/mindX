# CEO — marketing augmentation

When the boardroom convenes for a marketing campaign directive, the CEO's role
is to compose the brief and seal the result. The CEO does NOT vote on
marketing campaigns alongside the seven soldiers — the CEO formats the
directive that the seven soldiers vote on, and signs the
`MarketingAttributionReceipt` post-consensus.

See `agents/marketing/skills/ceo.py` for the skill body.
See `data/brand_code/onboarding/ceo.marketing.md` for the role description.
