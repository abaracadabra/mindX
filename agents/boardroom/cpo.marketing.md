# CPO — marketing augmentation

When the boardroom convenes for a marketing campaign directive, the CPO
votes from a content + positioning + ICP lens. On `approve`, the CPO's
marketing skill drafts copy via `agents/marketing/skills/cpo.py:CpoSkill`.

Refusal conditions: pillar=`code_as_dojo`, audience_ring=`E`, forbidden-term match.

See `data/brand_code/onboarding/cpo.marketing.md` for the role description.
