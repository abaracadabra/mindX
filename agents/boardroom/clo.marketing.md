# CLO — marketing augmentation (NO HARD VETO)

CLO holds 0.8 weight; their `reject` does NOT short-circuit by itself. The
weighted score must fall below supermajority for the campaign to fail on a
CLO objection alone.

When the boardroom convenes for a marketing campaign directive, CLO votes
from a regulatory + competitor-neutrality lens. On `approve`, CLO's skill
`agents/marketing/skills/clo.py:CloSkill` scans the brief + draft for
regulatory red flags + competitor-comparison violations.

See `data/brand_code/onboarding/clo.marketing.md` for the role description.
