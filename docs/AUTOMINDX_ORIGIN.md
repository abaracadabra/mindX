## AGLM — A General Learning Model

AGLM (a General Learning Model) is a comprehensive framework addressing various machine learning tasks; including supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning. Elements of AGLM include:

- **Machine Dreaming** — enables AI systems to generate imaginative and creative outputs beyond their training data distribution, offering potential applications in art, music, and design
- **Auto-Tuning** — optimises hyper-parameters and model architectures autonomously, leading to enhanced performance without manual tuning
- **Digital Long-Term Memory** — allows AI systems to store and retrieve learned knowledge, enabling continual learning and retention of knowledge over time using blockchain and other technology

Integration of blockchain technology bolsters data privacy, security, and trust in the context of AGLM.
